## Forks

Public forks are permitted under the Autark Community License.

However, forks must:

* Clearly identify themselves as unofficial.
* Remove official logos and branding.
* Not use Autark Labs trademarks as primary product branding.
* Avoid creating confusion regarding project ownership.

Examples:

Permitted:

* "My Custom Fork of Project OS"
* "Project OS Community Mod"

Not Permitted:
